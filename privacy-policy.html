We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.
